Common Questions from Clermont Homeowners

How long does a typical exterior painting project take?

Most exterior painting projects in Clermont take 3-4 days, depending on home size and weather conditions. We'll provide a specific timeline during your free estimate and keep you updated throughout the process.

Can you match my existing paint colors?

Yes! We can match virtually any existing color. We also offer color consultation services if you're looking to update your home's color scheme. With access to Sherwin-Williams' full palette, the options are endless.

Do I need to be home during the work?

Not necessarily. Many of our Clermont customers provide access and go about their day. We're trusted professionals who will treat your property with respect. However, you're always welcome to be present if you prefer.

What's included in your epoxy garage floor service?

Our epoxy floor service includes complete surface preparation (grinding and cleaning), crack repairs, professional epoxy application with decorative flakes, and a clear topcoat. We also offer a lifetime warranty on our garage floor work.
