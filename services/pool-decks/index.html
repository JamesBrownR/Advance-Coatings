Pool Deck Services

Transform your pool area with beautiful, slip-resistant coatings

Professional Pool Deck Coating Solutions

Your pool deck takes a beating from constant exposure to water, chlorine, UV rays, and foot traffic. Over time, even the best concrete can become faded, stained, and rough. A professional coating not only restores beauty to your pool area but also provides essential slip resistance for safety.

Advanced Coatings specializes in transforming worn pool decks into stunning outdoor spaces. With over 30 years of experience in Central Florida and exclusive use of Sherwin-Williams products, we deliver results that look great and stand up to daily use.

Our Pool Deck Services Include:

Non-Slip Coatings

Safety-first coatings with added grit to prevent slips when wet, meeting pool safety standards while looking great.

Decorative Finishes

Custom colors and textures to complement your pool and outdoor aesthetic, from cool deck effects to modern solid colors.

UV Protection

Florida-grade coatings that resist fading and degradation from constant sun exposure, keeping colors vibrant for years.

Chlorine Resistance

Specialized coatings formulated to withstand pool chemicals without deteriorating or discoloring over time.

Why Pool Deck Coating Matters

  • Safety First: Non-slip surface reduces accident risk, especially important with children
  • Heat Reduction: Cool deck coatings stay comfortable underfoot even in direct sunlight
  • Protects Concrete: Seals and protects against water damage, chlorine, and weathering
  • Hides Stains: Covers old oil, rust, and chemical stains for a fresh appearance
  • Increases Value: A beautiful pool area enhances your home's outdoor living space
  • Easy Maintenance: Sealed surfaces are easier to clean and maintain
