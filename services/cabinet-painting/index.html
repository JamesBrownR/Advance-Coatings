Professional Cabinet Refinishing

Dated cabinets can make your entire kitchen or bathroom feel old and tired. But replacement is expensive—often $15,000-$25,000 or more. Cabinet painting offers a stunning alternative that delivers the look of new cabinets at a fraction of the cost, typically saving you thousands of dollars.

Advanced Coatings specializes in cabinet refinishing with meticulous attention to detail. From careful surface preparation to flawless application, we transform your cabinets into beautiful, durable surfaces that look professionally done—because they are.

Our Process

1

Removal & Labeling

Remove all doors, drawers, and hardware, carefully labeling each piece for proper reinstallation.

2

Cleaning & Prep

Degrease, sand, and fill any imperfections to create a smooth, paint-ready surface.

3

Priming & Painting

Apply bonding primer followed by two coats of premium cabinet paint in your chosen color.

4

Reassembly

Reinstall doors, drawers, and hardware (new or existing) with proper alignment.
