Interior Painting Services

Transform your home with professional interior painting

Expert Interior Painting for Your Home

The right interior paint colors and professional application can completely transform how your home feels. Whether you're refreshing a single room or repainting your entire house, the quality of workmanship makes all the difference between a quick paint job and a beautiful, lasting finish.

Advanced Coatings brings over 30 years of interior painting expertise to every project. From careful surface preparation to meticulous application and cleanup, we treat your home with respect and deliver results that exceed expectations.

Our Interior Painting Services Include:

Whole House Painting

Complete interior transformation from bedrooms to kitchens, living rooms to hallways—cohesive, professional results throughout.

Room-by-Room Service

Individual room painting when you need to refresh specific spaces without a full house project.

Ceiling Painting

Professional ceiling application that eliminates streaks and lap marks for a smooth, uniform finish.

Trim & Molding

Detailed finishing work on baseboards, crown molding, door frames, and window trim for a polished look.

Why Choose Professional Interior Painting?

  • Proper Preparation: Surface prep, patching, and priming ensure paint adheres properly and lasts
  • Clean Lines: Professional cutting-in and edging creates crisp, straight lines
  • Even Coverage: No streaks, drips, or missed spots—just smooth, uniform color
  • Time Savings: We complete projects efficiently while you go about your life
  • Furniture Protection: Careful covering and protection of all furniture and floors
  • Color Expertise: Help choosing colors that work together and complement your home

Our Process

1

Consultation & Color Selection

We discuss your vision, offer color recommendations, and provide samples if needed.

2

Preparation

Move and cover furniture, patch holes, sand rough spots, and prime as needed.

3

Professional Application

Cut in edges, apply two coats with proper drying time between, ensuring even coverage.

4

Cleanup & Final Walkthrough

Remove all coverings, replace furniture, clean thoroughly, and ensure your complete satisfaction.

Popular Interior Paint Finishes

  • Flat/Matte: Best for ceilings and low-traffic areas, hides imperfections
  • Eggshell: Subtle sheen, great for living rooms and bedrooms
  • Satin: Easy to clean, ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and hallways
  • Semi-Gloss: Durable and washable, perfect for trim and high-traffic areas
