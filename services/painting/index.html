Our Painting Process

Professional results through proven methods

1

Free Estimate & Consultation

We assess your project, discuss colors and finishes, and provide a detailed estimate with no pressure or obligation.

2

Careful Preparation

Move and cover furniture, patch holes, sand surfaces, clean thoroughly, and prime as needed. Proper prep is critical.

3

Expert Application

Professional cutting-in, rolling, and detail work ensures even coverage with clean lines and smooth finish.

4

Thorough Cleanup

Remove all coverings and tape, clean floors and surfaces, replace furniture, and perform final quality check with you.
