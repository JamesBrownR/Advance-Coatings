Epoxy Countertop Services

Transform your countertops with beautiful, durable epoxy finishes

Professional Epoxy Countertop Resurfacing

Tired of dated, stained, or damaged countertops? Epoxy resurfacing offers an affordable, stunning alternative to complete replacement. We can transform your existing countertops into beautiful, durable surfaces that look like natural stone, marble, or create completely custom designs. At a fraction of the replacement cost.

Advanced Coatings specializes in epoxy countertop applications for kitchens, bathrooms, and commercial spaces. With over 30 years of experience and meticulous attention to detail, we deliver results that exceed expectations.

Why Choose Epoxy for Your Countertops?

  • Cost Effective: Save thousands compared to replacement
  • Seamless Surface: No grout lines or seams to trap dirt and bacteria
  • Heat Resistant: Withstands hot pots and pans without damage
  • Stain Resistant: Non-porous surface repels stains and spills
  • Easy to Clean: Simple soap and water keeps them looking new
  • Custom Designs: Unlimited color and pattern options
  • Quick Installation: Typically completed in 2-3 days

Popular Finishes

  • Stone Look: Replicate the look of granite, marble, or quartz
  • Solid Colors: Modern, clean monochromatic finishes
  • Metallic: Dramatic swirls and depth with metallic pigments
  • Terrazzo: Classic speckled stone appearance

Our Process

1

Surface Preparation

Thorough cleaning, sanding, and repair of existing countertops to create the perfect base.

2

Primer Application

Professional-grade primer ensures proper adhesion and longevity of the epoxy system.

3

Epoxy & Design

Apply epoxy in your chosen color/pattern, creating depth and visual interest.

4

Top Coat & Cure

Clear protective top coat provides durability and high-gloss finish. Proper curing ensures strength.
