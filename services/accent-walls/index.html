Accent Wall Services

Add character and style to any room with professional accent walls

Transform Rooms with Accent Walls

An accent wall is one of the most effective and affordable ways to add personality, depth, and visual interest to any room. Whether you want to highlight architectural features, create a focal point, or simply add a pop of color, a professionally painted accent wall makes a dramatic impact.

Advanced Coatings specializes in creating stunning accent walls that complement your home's style. From selecting the perfect color to crisp, clean application, we ensure your accent wall becomes a beautiful focal point that enhances your space.

Our Accent Wall Services Include:

Bold Color Walls

Statement walls in rich, saturated colors that add depth and personality to neutral rooms.

Geometric Patterns

Modern designs including stripes, chevrons, and custom geometric patterns for contemporary spaces.

Textured Finishes

Dimensional effects using specialty paints and techniques that add tactile interest.

Two-Tone Rooms

Sophisticated color blocking that divides rooms horizontally or vertically for visual drama.

Why Add an Accent Wall?

  • Creates Focal Points: Draws the eye and anchors room design
  • Adds Personality: Express your style without overwhelming the entire space
  • Defines Spaces: Visually separates areas in open-concept layouts
  • Cost-Effective Impact: Maximum visual effect with minimal paint required
  • Easy to Change: Update your look without repainting entire rooms
  • Highlights Architecture: Emphasizes fireplaces, built-ins, or unique features

Choosing the Right Wall

The best accent wall is typically:

  • The first wall you see when entering the room
  • The wall behind a bed, sofa, or fireplace
  • A wall with interesting architectural features
  • The longest uninterrupted wall in the room

Not sure which wall to choose? We'll assess your space during the free estimate and make recommendations.

Popular Accent Wall Ideas

  • Living Room: Behind the TV or sofa in a rich, warm tone
  • Bedroom: Behind the headboard in calming colors or dramatic darks
  • Dining Room: Bold jewel tones that create an intimate atmosphere
  • Home Office: Energizing colors that boost productivity
  • Nursery/Kids' Room: Playful colors or themed designs
