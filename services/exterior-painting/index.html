Exterior Painting Services

Protect and enhance your home with professional exterior painting

Expert Exterior House Painting

Your home's exterior takes a beating from Florida's intense sun, heavy rain, high humidity, and occasional hurricanes. Quality exterior paint isn't just about looks—it's your first line of defense against the elements. The right paint, properly applied, protects your investment and keeps your home looking beautiful for years.

Advanced Coatings has been painting homes throughout Central Florida for over 30 years. We use only Sherwin-Williams premium exterior paints, formulated specifically for Florida's challenging climate, and our meticulous preparation ensures results that last.

Our Exterior Painting Services Include:

Complete House Painting

Full exterior transformation including siding, trim, soffits, and fascia for a cohesive, fresh appearance.

Pressure Washing

Thorough cleaning to remove dirt, mold, mildew, and old chalking paint for proper adhesion.

Stucco Painting

Specialized application techniques for Florida's most common exterior surface, ensuring even coverage.

Trim & Accent Work

Detailed painting of shutters, doors, trim, and decorative elements that add character to your home.

Why Professional Exterior Painting Matters

  • Weather Protection: Seals and protects against moisture, preventing rot and structural damage
  • UV Resistance: Quality paint resists fading and degradation from constant Florida sun
  • Mold & Mildew Prevention: Proper paint systems resist growth in humid conditions
  • Curb Appeal: Fresh paint dramatically improves your home's appearance
  • Increased Value: Well-maintained exteriors boost property values
  • Long-Lasting Results: Professional application means 7-10+ year lifespan

Our Process

1

Color Consultation

We help you choose colors that complement your home's architecture and neighborhood.

2

Pressure Washing & Prep

Deep clean all surfaces, scrape loose paint, caulk gaps, and repair any damaged areas.

3

Priming & Painting

Apply primer to bare wood, then two coats of premium paint for maximum protection.

4

Cleanup & Inspection

Remove all coverings, clean work areas, and perform final quality inspection with you.

Sherwin-Williams Exterior Paint Systems

We exclusively use Sherwin-Williams paints because they're specifically formulated for durability and performance in harsh Florida conditions. Their acrylic latex systems provide superior adhesion, flexibility, and fade resistance.
