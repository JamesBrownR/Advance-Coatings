Epoxy Flooring Services

Transform your garage or workspace with durable, beautiful epoxy floors

Professional Epoxy Floor Coating

Epoxy flooring transforms ordinary concrete into a durable, attractive surface that's perfect for garages, workshops, and commercial spaces. Unlike paint, epoxy creates a thick, glossy coating that bonds chemically to concrete, providing superior durability and resistance to chemicals, stains, and wear.

At Advanced Coatings, we've installed epoxy floors throughout Central Florida for over 30 years. Using professional-grade Sherwin-Williams epoxy systems, we deliver showroom-quality results that protect your investment and look great for years.

Benefits of Epoxy Flooring

  • Extreme Durability: Resists impacts, abrasion, and heavy traffic far better than concrete or paint
  • Chemical Resistant: Impervious to oil, gasoline, brake fluid, and most household chemicals
  • Easy to Clean: Smooth, non-porous surface wipes clean in minutes
  • Bright & Reflective: Glossy finish reflects light, making spaces brighter
  • Long Lasting: Properly installed epoxy floors last 10-20+ years
  • Increases Property Value: Professional epoxy floors are a selling point for homes

Color & Finish Options

We offer multiple epoxy systems to suit your style and budget:

  • Solid Color: Classic chip-style epoxy in a variety of colors
  • Flake System: Decorative flakes for texture and visual interest
  • Metallic: Premium metallic finishes with unique swirled patterns
  • Custom Blends: Mix colors to perfectly match your space

Our Installation Process

1

Floor Preparation

Diamond grinding or shot blasting to create the perfect profile for epoxy adhesion. This step is critical for longevity.

2

Crack Repair

Fill and level cracks, divots, and damaged areas to create a smooth, uniform surface.

3

Epoxy Application

Professional application of primer coat, base coat, and decorative flakes or metallic pigments.

4

Top Coat & Cure

Clear polyurethane or polyaspartic top coat for maximum protection and UV resistance. Proper curing ensures durability.
