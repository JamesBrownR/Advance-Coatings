Deck Painting Services

Protect and beautify your deck with professional painting and staining

Professional Deck Restoration & Painting

Your deck is constantly exposed to Florida's intense sun, heavy rain, and humidity. Without proper protection, wood deteriorates, paint peels, and your outdoor living space becomes an eyesore. Professional deck painting and staining not only restores beauty but provides essential protection against the elements.

Advanced Coatings has been restoring decks throughout Central Florida for over 30 years. Using premium Sherwin-Williams deck coatings and stains, we deliver results that protect your investment and enhance your outdoor living experience.

Our Deck Services Include:

Deck Painting

Solid color deck paint that covers old stains and creates a uniform, protective surface that lasts for years.

Deck Staining

Transparent or semi-transparent stains that enhance natural wood grain while providing UV and moisture protection.

Waterproof Sealing

Premium sealants that protect against water damage, rot, and mildew in Florida's humid climate.

Railing Painting

Complete deck finishing including railings, balusters, and trim work for a cohesive, professional look.

Why Professional Deck Coating Matters

  • Weather Protection: Shields wood from sun, rain, and humidity damage
  • Prevents Rot: Waterproof barrier stops moisture penetration and wood decay
  • Splinter Prevention: Smooth finish eliminates rough, splintery surfaces
  • Extends Deck Life: Proper coating can double or triple your deck's lifespan
  • Enhances Beauty: Fresh color or natural wood enhancement transforms your outdoor space
  • Increases Home Value: Well-maintained decks are a major selling point

Our Process

1

Inspection & Prep

We inspect for damage, make necessary repairs, and thoroughly pressure wash to remove old finishes and dirt.

2

Sanding & Repair

Sand rough areas, replace damaged boards if needed, and ensure a smooth surface for coating.

3

Painting or Staining

Professional application of premium deck coating in your chosen color and finish.

4

Sealing & Finishing

Apply protective topcoat for maximum durability and weather resistance.

Stain vs. Paint: Which is Right for You?

Choose Stain if: You want to showcase natural wood grain, prefer a more natural look, or have high-quality wood.

Choose Paint if: You want complete coverage, need to hide imperfections, or prefer bold color options.

Not sure? We'll assess your deck and recommend the best option during your free estimate.
