Meet the Owner

Mark McCormick

30+ Years in the Business

How It All Started

Mark McCormick has been in the painting and coating business for over 30 years. He didn't start Advanced Coatings because he wanted to run the biggest operation in Florida—he started it because he wanted to do good work and build something he could be proud of.

Like a lot of contractors, Mark started small. Just him, his tools, and a work truck. Back in those early days, it was mostly residential painting jobs—nothing fancy. But what set Mark apart was how he treated each project. He showed up on time, did thorough prep work, and didn't leave until the job was done right. Word got around, and before long, neighbors were calling him because someone down the street had recommended him.

That's still how the business works today—one quality job leading to another.

What Makes Advanced Coatings Different

Over three decades, Mark's learned that most homeowners don't just want their house painted—they want someone who actually cares about the end result. They want someone who won't cut corners, who'll use materials that last, and who won't disappear when the job gets tough.

That's where the "Beyond Painting" approach comes in. Mark treats every surface like it's going to need to stand up to Florida's brutal sun, humidity, and afternoon thunderstorms. Because it will. He's seen what happens when contractors rush through prep work or use cheap products, and he's built his reputation on doing the opposite.

Whether it's an epoxy garage floor, a freshly painted exterior, or a refinished pool deck, Mark approaches each job the same way: like it's his own house. That means proper surface prep, quality Sherwin-Williams products, and attention to the little details that most people miss.

He's built relationships with homeowners who've trusted him not just once, but for multiple projects over the years. That kind of trust doesn't come from flashy advertising—it comes from consistently doing what you said you'd do.
